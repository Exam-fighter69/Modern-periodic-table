Thallium

Thallium is a chemical element with the symbol Tl and atomic number 81. It is a soft, silvery-white metal that belongs to the group of elements known as the post-transition metals. Thallium is a highly toxic element and is classified as a heavy metal. It is used in some electronic devices, as well as in the manufacture of certain types of glass, lenses, and semiconductors.
