Erbium

Erbium is a chemical element with the symbol Er and atomic number 68. It is a silvery-white rare earth metal that is soft and malleable. It was discovered by Swedish chemist Carl Gustaf Mosander in 1843. Erbium is found in minerals such as gadolinite, monazite, and xenotime, and is extracted



Physical properties of Erbium are :

  1. Erbium is a silver-colored metal that belongs to the lanthanide series of the periodic table.
  2. In pure form it is malleable, soft and stable in air. It is not quickly oxidize as some other rare earth metals
  3. Its salts color are rose colored. It has sharp absorption spectra bands in visible light
  4. It is ferromagnetic below 19 K and antiferromagnetic between 19 and 80 K. Erbium is paramagnetic above 80 K
