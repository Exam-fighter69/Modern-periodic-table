Platinum

Platinum is a precious metal that is known for its durability, resistance to corrosion, and rarity. It is a dense, malleable, and ductile metal that is silvery-white in color. Platinum is often used in jewelry and is considered one of the most valuable and luxurious metals. It is also used in various industrial applications such as catalytic converters, electrical contacts, and laboratory equipment.



Physical properties of Platinum are :

  1. Platinum is a dense, malleable, and ductile metal with a silvery-white color.
  2. The element is also highly resistant to corrosion and oxidation
  3. Platinum is a good conductor of electricity and heat.
  4. it's often described as a noble metal because it is so unreactive.
