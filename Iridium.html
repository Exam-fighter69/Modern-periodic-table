Iridium

Iridium is a chemical element with the symbol Ir and atomic number 77. It is a dense, silvery-white metal that is known for its extreme resistance to corrosion and high melting point. Iridium is a member of the platinum group of elements and is found in platinum ores.



Physical properties of Iridium are :

  1. Iridium is a hard, brittle, lustrous, dense, transition metal of the platinum family.
  2. It is unaffected by air, water and acids.
  3. It is silvery-white and it is notable for being the most corrosion resistant element known.
