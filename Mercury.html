Mercury

Mercury is a chemical element with the symbol W and atomic number 74. It is a dense, silvery-white metal with high melting and boiling points, making it a heavy, durable, and heat-resistant material. Mercury is also known for its exceptional strength and hardness, making it useful in a variety of industrial applications.



Physical properties of Mercury are :

  1. Mercury has a melting point of -38.9oC, a boiling point of 356.7oC, and is the only metal to remain in liquid form at room temperature.
  2. Mercury is a unique metallic element that has several distinctive physical properties
  3. Mercury is a poor conductor of heat, but an excellent conductor of electricity.
  4. It has a high surface tension
