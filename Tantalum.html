Tantalum

Tantalum is a chemical element with the symbol Ta and atomic number 73. It is a rare, hard, blue-gray, lustrous transition metal that is highly corrosion-resistant and has a high melting point. The metal is also known for its ability to store and discharge electrical charges, which makes it a valuable component in electronics such as capacitors.
