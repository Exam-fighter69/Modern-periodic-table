Tungsten

Tungsten is a chemical element with the symbol W and atomic number 74. It is a dense, silvery-white metal with high melting and boiling points, making it a heavy, durable, and heat-resistant material. Tungsten is also known for its exceptional strength and hardness, making it useful in a variety of industrial applications.



Physical properties of Tungsten are :

  1. Tungsten is a greyish-white lustrous metal, which is a solid at room temperature.
  2. Tungsten has a high tensile strength and is very resistant to corrosion and oxidation
  3. It is also a good conductor of heat and electricity.
  4. It is a very dense and hard metal, with a grayish-white color.
